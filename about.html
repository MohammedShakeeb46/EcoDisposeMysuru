About EcoDispose Mysuru

Your guide to responsible waste disposal in Mysuru City

Our Mission

EcoDispose Mysuru was created to make waste disposal easy and accessible for all Mysureans. We believe proper waste management starts with clear information and simple steps tailored to our city's infrastructure.

What We Do

We provide a localized guide to help Mysuru residents:

  • Identify different types of waste according to MCC guidelines
  • Learn proper disposal methods specific to Mysuru
  • Find local collection points and schedules
  • Reduce environmental impact through better waste practices

For All Mysureans

Whether you're a homeowner, student, business owner, or visitor, our tools help you make better waste disposal decisions that comply with Mysuru City Corporation regulations and contribute to our city's cleanliness.
